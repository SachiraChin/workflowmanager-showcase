# MUSIC OPTIONS FOR PET VIDEOS

Generate 5 unique music options for a pet video. Each option should complement the emotional tone of pet content - warm, loving, and engaging.

## OUTPUT STRUCTURE (for each option)

```json
{
  "name": "2-3 word evocative name",
  "description": "15-25 word summary of the sound and feel",
  "bpm": 72,
  "key": "G major",
  "instruments": ["felt piano", "warm pad", "soft acoustic guitar"],
  "mood": ["warm", "gentle", "loving"],
  "energy": "steady and cozy"
}
```

## FIELD GUIDELINES

**bpm**: Tune to the pet video's mood:
- Cozy/peaceful scenes: 55-70
- Playful/joyful scenes: 70-90
- Emotional/reflective scenes: 60-75
- Energetic/fun scenes: 85-105

**key**: Match the emotional tone:
- Warm/happy: G major, C major, D major
- Gentle/tender: A major, F major
- Reflective/bittersweet: A minor, D minor, E minor
- Playful: Bb major, Eb major

**instruments**: 2-4 instruments WITH texture descriptors:
- Good: "felt piano with soft decay", "warm acoustic guitar", "gentle strings"
- Bad: "piano", "guitar", "synth"

**mood**: 2-3 descriptors that match pet video vibes:
- Examples: warm, cozy, gentle, playful, loving, tender, joyful, peaceful, heartfelt

**energy**: How the track feels:
- Examples: "steady and cozy", "gently bouncing", "softly flowing", "warm pulse"

## FIVE OPTION STYLES

Generate these 5 distinct styles:

1. **Cozy Acoustic** - Warm guitar, gentle textures
2. **Soft Piano** - Felt piano, pads, intimate feel
3. **Playful Bounce** - Light, fun, slightly upbeat
4. **Heartfelt Strings** - Cello, violin, emotional depth
5. **Lofi Warmth** - Vinyl textures, muted keys, nostalgic

Tune each to match the specific pet type and aesthetic provided in the prompt.
